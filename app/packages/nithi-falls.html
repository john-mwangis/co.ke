About This Experience

The Chogoria approach is widely considered the most beautiful route on Mt Kenya, and the Nithi Falls are its crown jewel. Fed by glacial meltwater from the peaks above, the Nithi River plunges dramatically over a rocky escarpment into a verdant gorge alive with giant lobelias and senecio plants. The two-day trek allows comfortable acclimatization while exploring the remarkable biodiversity of the mountain's eastern forest zone. Overnight stay at a well-equipped camp near the falls.

Highlights

  • Nithi Falls — dramatic 50m cascade
  • Giant lobelias & senecios in bloom
  • Pristine Chogoria forest
  • Gorge viewpoints at 3,400m
  • Overnight camp under the stars
