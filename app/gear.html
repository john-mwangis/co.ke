🛏 Shelter & Sleeping

  • ▸Sleeping bag (rated to -10°C / 14°F)
  • ▸Sleeping mat / sleeping pad

🥾 Footwear

  • ▸Sturdy waterproof hiking boots (ankle support)
  • ▸Gaiters (short trail gaiters)

🧭 Safety & Navigation

  • ▸Headlamp with fresh batteries (+ spares)
  • ▸First aid kit (personal)
  • ▸Blister treatment (moleskin, Compeed)
  • ▸Sunscreen SPF 50+ (UV radiation doubles above 3,500m)
  • ▸UV-blocking sunglasses
  • + 1 more essentials

💧 Hydration & Nutrition

  • ▸Water bottles or hydration reservoir (3L total)
  • ▸Water purification tablets or filter
  • ▸Electrolyte powder / sachets
  • ▸High-energy snacks (nuts, bars, chocolate)

🎒 Personal Items

  • ▸Backpack 35–50L (trekking pack)
  • ▸Dry bags / pack liner (waterproof)
  • ▸Passport & KWS entry permits
  • ▸Travel insurance documents (mountain rescue cover required)
  • ▸Lip balm with SPF
  • + 2 more essentials

⚠ Critical: Sleeping Bag

Temperatures at Mackinder's Camp (4,200m) regularly drop to -5°C to -10°C at night. A sleeping bag rated to -10°C is not optional — it is essential for your safety.

💧 Hydration at Altitude

Drink 3–4 litres of water daily above 3,000m. Dehydration significantly increases the risk of altitude sickness. Our guides monitor this — please follow their guidance.
