Mt Kenya Trail Map

Navigate the routes, huts, and phases of Mt Kenya National Park.

North Phase

Sirimon Route

The driest, most wildlife-rich approach. Passes Old Moses Camp (3,300m) and Shipton's Camp (4,200m).

▸ Old Moses Camp
▸ Shipton's Camp
2–3 days to summit area
South Phase

Naro Moru Route

The most direct and popular southern approach. Famous "vertical bog" and Meteorological Station.

▸ Met Station (3,050m)
▸ Mackinder's Camp (4,200m)
2–3 days to summit area
Eastern Terminal

Chogoria Route

The most scenic route on Mt Kenya — passes Nithi Falls and the stunning Lake Michaelson.

▸ Meru Mt Kenya Lodge
▸ Lake Ellis
▸ Lake Michaelson Camp
3–4 days to summit area
Western Terminal

Burgureti Route

A remote and less-traveled western approach through dense forest and open moorlands.

▸ Liki North Camp
▸ Mackinder's Camp (junction)
3–4 days to summit area
